About Journey to Horizon Services

We are a registered NDIS provider committed to delivering high-quality support services tailored to your individual needs.

Our Mission

  • To empower, encourage and enable people who have disabilities to enhance their quality of life with dignity & respect
  • To evaluate and exceed stakeholders’ expectations
  • To provide care focused on the holistic view of an individual

Our Vision

Journey to Horizon Services Pty Ltd’s vision is to create a resilient and empowered community in partnership to supporting people with disability to live a great life with choice.


Our Values

  • Empowerment
  • Dignity
  • Respect
  • Trust
  • Integrity
  • Innovation

Our Services

We offer a range of NDIS services to support your independence and wellbeing.

Daily Living Support

Assistance with daily activities to help you maintain independence in your own home, including personal care, meal preparation, and household tasks.

Community Participation

Support to engage in social and community activities, develop skills, and build relationships to enhance your quality of life.

Capacity Building

Programs designed to develop your skills and independence, including support coordination, therapy services, and skill development.

Assistive Technology

Assessment, recommendation, and support for equipment and technology that increases your independence and participation.

Transport Services

Safe and reliable transportation to help you attend appointments, social activities, and community engagements.

Respite Care

Temporary care options to give primary caregivers a break while ensuring participants receive quality support.
